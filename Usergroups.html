Cultural Travelers

Exploring the history,
art, and customs of your destination is likely a top priority if you're interested in cultural travel.
Visiting museums, landmarks, and attending cultural events can provide a deeper understanding of the local culture.
Interacting with locals can offer a unique and enriching experience

Nature Travelers

Hiking, birdwatching, wildlife safaris, and scenic drives
are some of your favorite activities.
Seeking out destinations that offer national parks,
beaches, mountains, or forests allows you to immerse yourself
in the beauty of nature.
