AboutMe

Hermoni Granger(Blogger)

Thanks for looking! I'm David Leiter, and this is my travel blog.

I met my sweet Boyfriend and full time travel buddy Intan while I was traveling in Asia in 2017, and we got married two years later on a beach in Bali, Indonesia.

She's a former English teacher, makeup artist and homegrown Balinese. Her name means diamond. She grew up riding a scooter and she's tri-lingual (speaks 3 languages fluently).

She's been an indispensable translator for me in remote parts of Indonesia where almost no one speaks English, like Banggai, Labengki, and the Banyak Islands.

We've been traveling the world full time as a couple for 5+ years now, and we spend a lot of time on her home island Bali. We have some more big international trips planned for 2024.

We hope you'll tag along! Please stay tuned!
