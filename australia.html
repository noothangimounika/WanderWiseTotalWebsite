Authentic Fijian Day Cruise, Best Day in Fiji

The Mamanuca Islands can only be reached by boat, so take the stress out of transport planning by having everything organized for you. After a Denarau Island hotel pickup, you’ll board the boat and head to Sand Island, Monu Island, and Modriki Island. Seeing multiple islands in one day means you’ll make the most of your trip and cover more ground. Enjoy free time to swim and snorkel, and a delicious Fijian buffet lunch. Read more about Authentic Fijian Day Cruise, Best Day in Fiji

Your Mamanuca Islands adventure begins with a convenient pickup from Denarau Island hotels, ensuring a hassle-free start to your day. Board the boat and set sail to Mana Sand the island, where you can swim and snorkel in the crystal-clear waters. Read more about Authentic Fijian Day Cruise, Best Day in Fiji

Malamala Beach Club - Full Day

Relax on a private island with this day pass to Malamala Beach Club. Depart from Port Denarau as you enjoy a brief ferry transfer across crystal clear water. Spend a full day doing whatever you want to do while you're there, including the use of non-motorized water sports equipment, or indulge in chef-made poolside snacks with a selection of cocktails, wines, and beer (own expense). Read more about Malamala Beach Club - Full Day

Surrounded by Fiji’s famous crystal clear waters, Malamala Beach Club introduces you to the world’s first beach club located on its very own island, just 25 minutes from Port Denarau. Enjoy the beach club with a day pass and experience white sand beaches, beachside cabanas, a resort-style infinity-edge pool, and views across the Mamanucas from Fiji’s best-located island. Malamala Beach Club offers a delicious a-la-carte menu from light bites to tame your craving or pacific-inspired meals and shared platters, an array of hand-crafted cocktails, and a club like atmosphere with upbeat tunes mixed all day long. Read more about Malamala Beach Club - Full Day

Full Day South Sea Island Day Cruise

Enjoy the ultimate day out in paradise at South Sea Island, in Fiji’s Mamanuca archipelago. After a cruise from Port Denarau, arrive at your tropical destination and choose from various options. Snorkel to discover the stunning coral reefs, kayak in the lagoons, ride a semi-submersible vessel, or upgrade to scuba dive. Feast on a barbecue lunch with unlimited beer, wine, and soft drinks throughout the day. Read more about Full Day South Sea Island Day Cruise

South Sea Island—part of Fiji's Mamanuca Islands—is a small piece of paradise a short sail from Port Denarau. Spend a full day on South Sea Island and find plenty to do with unlimited use of the island facilities. After a hotel pickup from Nadi, Denarau, or the airport, you’re transferred to Port Denarau to board your vessel for the short cruise. On arrival, spend your time relaxing or don a mask and flippers for fabulous snorkeling opportunities, even at low tide. Read more about Full Day South Sea Island Day Cruise

South Sea Island Half Day Cruise

Sail the gorgeous South Sea Island nestled among the Mamanuca Islands. Just thirty minutes from Denarau, explore crystal waters, coral reefs, and swim among the colorful tropical fish. Adventure comes to you with access to watersport equipment, dance performances, and a subsea adventure in the semi-submersible coral viewer. Indulge in the included lunch, alcoholic beverages, and bottled water. The perfect opportunity to relax and experience the best of Fiji. Read more about South Sea Island Half Day Cruise

Half a day to South Sea Island is an easy 30-minute ferry ride. Perfect for those who don't have a lot of time to spare, but still want a taste of the 'real Fiji'. This fantastic cruise means you'll get to explore one of Fiji's cutest Mamanuca Islands and get into 'island time' The island will be yours for the rest of the day until the catamaran arrives around 5.30 pm to bring you back to Port Denarau. Read more about South Sea Island Half Day Cruise

Ultimate Encounter Snorkel with Sharks in Fiji

Get access to an incredible encounter with marine life on this ultimate snorkel with sharks encounter in Fiji. It's a hassle-free day with a full itinerary organized for you—you are picked up from your hotel and taken by boat to Kuata Island. This tour is suitable for all skill levels, including first-time snorkelers, and you get a full safety briefing. After an amazing shark encounter, lunch is included too. Read more about Ultimate Encounter Snorkel with Sharks in Fiji

Departing Port Denarau daily you'll board our high-speed catamaran and travel through the Mamanuca Islands bound for Barefoot Kuata Resort in the southern Yasawa Islands. Kuata Island boasts a picture-perfect beach, transparent turquoise waters, unusual rock formations and the same warm hospitality synonymous with Fijian people. There is rustic 'Robinson Crusoe' styled accommodation with the comforts of a restaurant, bar, hammocks and three swimming pools. Following a briefing on the beachfront, you’ll board a boat with your guides and travel to our snorkelling with reef sharks site.
